About Me

My name is Bhreigha Paul and I'm originally from Peoria, AZ. I've lived in Arizona my entire life and grew up in the west valley. I lived in Prescott, AZ for 2.5 years while getting my Associate's degree in Criminal Justice before moving to Glendale, AZ.

It wasn't until I was offered a job at Carvana's HQ in Tempe, AZ that I had the potential to pursue an actual career again. I currently work in the titles department at the company, but I have big plans for myself once I receive my certificate from this bootcamp.

My goals are to get my certificate, apply for an engineering position at my workplace, and have a career in IT as either a front or back end engineer, or maybe even both. I really enjoy working at Carvana and I see myself staying with the company for many years. Plus it doesn't hurt to make myself more valuable by furthing my education.
